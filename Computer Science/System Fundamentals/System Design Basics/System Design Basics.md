**Hardware** - The physical elements of a computer
**Software** - A series of instructions that can be understood by a CPU
**Peripheral Device** - Any auxiliary device that can communicate and work with a computer (i/o devices)
**Computer Network** - a set of computer systems that are interconnected and share resources, as well as data (LANs, WANs, etc)
**Human Resources** - People who are used or could be used in an organization, business or economy
