If $P(x)$ is divided by $ax+b$ until a constant remainder $R$ is obtained, then
$$\frac{P(x)}{D(x)}=Q(x)+\frac{R(x)}{D(x)}$$
where $D(x)$ is the *divisor*, $Q(x)$ is the quotient, $R(x)$ is the remainder

ergo, $P(x)=Q(x)\cdot D(x)+R(x)$
