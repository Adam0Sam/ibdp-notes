### The graph of $y=f(|x|)$

From the [[Absolute Values, Modulus|definition of what a modulus]] is 
$$f(|x|) =
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{}
f(x) \quad \text{if} \ x\ge0 \\
f(-x) \quad \text{if} \ x\le0
\end{array}
\right. $$
