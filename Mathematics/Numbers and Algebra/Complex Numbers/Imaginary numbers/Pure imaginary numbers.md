The formal definition: _An imaginary number is a [[Complex Numbers|complex number]] $a+bi$, where $a=0$_

These come in unsimplified and simplified forms

| Unsimplified form | Simplified form |
| ----------------- | --------------- |
| $\sqrt{-9}$       | $3i$            |
| $\sqrt{-5}$       | $i\sqrt{5}$     |
| $-\sqrt{-144}$    | $-12i$          |
