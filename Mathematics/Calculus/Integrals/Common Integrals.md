Commonly observed functions
$$
\begin{align}
&\int k \, dx =kx+c \\
&\int x^{n} \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+c \quad\text{where } n\ne-1 \\
&\int e^{x} \, dx =e^{x} +c\\
&\int a^{x}\ln a \, dx = a^{x}+c \\
 & \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln |x|+c \\
 & \int  \frac{1}{x\ln a} \, dx = \log_{a}|x| +c
\end{align}
$$
